About

Hi, I'm Katie. For 15 years I've built content ecosystems for B2B and SaaS brands — as an executive editor, a content director, and a community lead for a global tech audience. Most recently, I spearheaded The CTO Club from launch to $40K+ in weekly revenue, 1.9M active visitors in year one, and 38% average newsletter open rates as a solo shop, owning strategy, workflows, and execution across every channel.

I'm a true Xennial: nostalgic for analog life, all in on digital, and riding the AI wave without losing sight of the humans it's supposed to serve. I build structured, human-in-the-loop AI workflows that let lean teams produce more (without sacrificing editorial judgment, brand voice, or quality).

I'm based in Raleigh, NC, and work with clients remotely, on a fractional or project basis.

Copyediting & Editorial QA

Community Building & Support
